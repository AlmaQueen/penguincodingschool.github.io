The Tiger Duchess Rising

In a new town, a girl with extraordinary powers walks to school completely unaware of them.

The adventures that await her are unimaginable, and they all start with a creepy visit to a haunted house....

If you want to learn more about the girl and her powers, read on!

Summary

The girl you read about was Elaina Dogwood. She lives with her aunt,but her parents come to her in her dreams. One day her parents tell her that she will move to a town called Benton. There, she would find a house that is supposedly haunted. Her parents continually delivered this message. Suddenly, her aunt decided that they woud move. Everything her parents ever told her was beginning to come true! Elaina was getting dangerously close to figuring out the truth of why her parents left her, and every step took her closer to discovering her powers.
