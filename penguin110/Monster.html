Monster

A monster is Sonic's term to describe Eggface's transportation, Metal Sonic's friends, and Metal Sonic's monsterous forms

Description

Although monsters were created by Eggface, they're made to kill other Clan people by attack. Regardless of the killing, they also crush people like Warriors Fire and Ice and Rising Storm. In those two books, Cinderpaw disobeyed Fireheart and she was also hit by a monster. Unfortunately, Cinderpaw was severely maimed for life. In the Rising Storm, Whitethroat was hit by a monster and also died instantly. Cloudpaw was tooken away in the belly of a monster. In Sonic the Hedgehog Jedi #6, Melodypaw's aunt and uncle were both hit by a monster. Sonic believes that monsters have round black paws that smell like burning stone with yellow beams shinining from their eyes and also go high speed. monsters have different weapons to kill people. The Death Egg Robot is a tree-eater. The Sonic Unleashed 6 bosses are air monsters. The Nega Wisp Armor is a wisp-eater. Metal Madness and Overlord are air monsters.
