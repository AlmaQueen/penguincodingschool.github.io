In greek mythology there are the 12 main gods. these gods are called the 12 olympians. Below are the 12 olympians names,powers, and what they represent:

  • ZEUS,controls air,king of the gods,ruler of the sky
  • HERA,goddess of marriage and family,queen of the gods, represented ideal woman
  • poseidon,god of sea,earthquakes and horses.One of the big six
  • DEMETER,one of the big six,goddess of harvest, agriculture,she presided over the sacred law and the cycle of life and death.she symbols earths powers over living things
  • ARES,the god of war, precisely the god of warlike frenzy.he represents violent and physical aspect of war
  • ATHENA,the goddess of war, wisdom, courage, inspiration, civilazation, justice, math, strategy,arts,and skill, she is the defender of cities and protectress of heroes.people think of her as the female counterpart of ares,she represents the strategic aspect of war.
  • APOLO,the god of music,truth,prophecy,healig,sun and light,plague and poetry
  • ARTEMIS,the goddess of the wilderness,the hunt,and wild animals.she is the helper of midwives as a goddess of birth.she is a symbol of the moon and the evening
  • HEPHAESTUS,the god of fire,crafts and the god of blacksmiths
  • APHRODITE,goddess of love,beauty, and fertility.she was the protectress of heroes.
  • HERMES,the god of commerce.he was the messenger of the gods and the link between humans and the greek gods.he was the protector of travelers,thieves,and athletes
  • DIONYSUS,the god of grape harvest,wine making and wine
