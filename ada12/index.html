Snow Leopard Wonderland

This website is about Snow Leopards.

Snow Leopards are endangered animals. There are very few of these majestic creatures left in this world, so we should do everything we can to help them. There are about 3500 to 7000 snow leopards left in the world. to learn more about Snow Leopards click here

Snow Leopards live in very snowy, mountainous regions. They are well adapted to their environment. For example they have short forelimbs, good sized paws and long sized paws to help them climb mountains.
