Board of Selectmen

The elected, five member Board of Selectmen meets throughout the year to make policy and oversee the operations of Town government, pursuant to the Selectmen-Town Manager Act of 1968. The Selectmen have weekly office hours to meet with interested citizens to receive comments, suggestions, and concerns. These are held in the Selectmens' Office on the second floor of the Town Office Building.
