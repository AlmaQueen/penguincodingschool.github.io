Daniel's Game/Bird of Prey Website.

This is my Game/Bird of Prey Website.

The Controls for the Game are W to Jump,
A to go Left, S to drop down through platforms,
and D to go Right.
The Carrot is the Coin.
Each Carrot/Coin Gives you 10 points, which are listed in the Score.
To Beat the Level/Survive in the Food Chain, you Must Get a Score of 100.
If You Were Wondering, Your Character is a Rabbit/Bunny and the Monster/Predator is a Harpy Eagle.
