Where Bonobos live:

Bonobos live in a small area around the Congo Basin in the Democratic Republic of the Congo, in Central Africa

What Bonobos Eat:

Bonobos are Omnivorus, meaning they eat both plants and animals. They eat things such as fruit, leaves, eggs, and meat from small animals
