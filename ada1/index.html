Warrior Cats: All You Want To Know!

This website will teach you all about the warrior cats from Warriors by Erin Hunter.

Clans

There are four main clans in warriors; ThunderClan, ShadowClan, RiverClan, and WindClan. They each ahve territories and hunting grounds in a camp. There are dens for different groups of cats.
