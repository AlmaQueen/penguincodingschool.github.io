Porpoise

Introduction:

This website captures the importance of Porpoises and their unique abilities. It explores the endangerment of them and how to put a stop to it.

Vaquita

The Vaquita Porpoises are critically endangered. There are only about 30 left in the wild. Fishermen (and women) that fish using gill nets sometimes catch porpoises who are trying to simply catch themselves a dinner and get the porpoises killed due to them drowning in the nets. A few years ago there were 60 Vaquitas left in the wild but the poputation has dropped dirasticly. The Vaquita is the most endangered porpoise out of them all yet sadly, one of the cutest.

Fun Fact

The name Vaquita means Little Cow.

Harbor

The harbor porpoise (aka the common porpoise) is probably the most common species of porpoise. They live in the temprate costal waters of the Northern Hemisphere.
