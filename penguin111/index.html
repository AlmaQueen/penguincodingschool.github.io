The Warriors - The Prophecies Begin

This Website

On this website, you will learn about the characters in The Warriors by Erin Hunter. There are five series all together, but this is about the first one called, The Prophecies Begin. There are six books in thiss series, listed below.

Introduction

The Warriors is about a cat named Rusty who is a house cat. One day, he meets cats from the forest who invite him to join the in the wild. But, once he makes the decision, he cannot change his mind. Rusty makes the decision to join them, and his greatest adventures have just begun!

In the wild, there are five clans: Thunderclan, Riverclan, Windclan, Shadowclan, and Starclan. Unlike the others, Starclan is located up in the stars, and is the clan where cats go when they die. It is where their ancestors live. The four clans are rivals, but they all worship Starclan. The main character lives in Thunderclan.

More Books
