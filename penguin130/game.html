Find The Bone

This game is about a dog who put his bone somewhere in his yard but now he can't find it again! He follows the smell carefully until he discovers his bone! Click around the picture. The hotter it gets, the closer you are to the bone. Get as close to 0 pixles away as you can. When you get close enough, you will see a congradulations screen and the game is over. Then the dog dog loses the bone again and needs help finding it!
